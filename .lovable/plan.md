

# Reve Living - Premium UK Furniture E-Commerce Website

## Overview
A luxury furniture e-commerce website for the UK market, featuring beds, mattresses, and bedroom furniture. The design combines modern elegance with warm bronze accents, smooth animations, and a premium shopping experience.

---

## Design System

### Color Palette
- **Primary Bronze:** #B07A3E (CTAs, accents)
- **Deep Bronze:** #8B5E34 (hover states, borders)
- **Espresso Brown:** #4A3A2E (text, footer)
- **Soft Ivory Cream:** #F9F4EE (backgrounds)
- **Warm Off-White:** #F7F2EA (cards, dropdowns)

### Visual Elements
- Custom animated cursor (expands on text hover with blend mode)
- Framer Motion animations throughout
- Smooth page transitions
- Elegant hover effects on products and links

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Homepage
- **Announcement Bar** - Bronze bar with delivery/promo message
- **Header** - Logo, navigation menu, search, cart icon
- **Hero Slider** - Full-width image carousel with bedroom scenes
- **Shop by Category** - Visual tiles for each bed type
- **Bestsellers Grid** - Featured products with quick view
- **Trust Section** - UK Handcrafted & Payment Flexibility badges
- **Customer Reviews** - Testimonial carousel
- **Inspiration Gallery** - Lifestyle bedroom images
- **Newsletter Signup** - Email capture with bronze CTA
- **Footer** - Navigation, contact info, payment icons

### 2. Category Pages
- **Divan Beds** - With drawer storage, headboard variations
- **Ottoman Beds** - Lift-up storage option
- **Upholstered Beds** - Premium fabric beds
- **Wooden Beds** - Classic timber frames
- **Bunk Beds** - Kids and guest options
- **Mattresses** - Various comfort levels

Each category includes:
- Hero banner with category description
- Filter sidebar (size, color, price range)
- Sort options (price, popularity, newest)
- Product grid with hover animations

### 3. Product Detail Page
- Large image gallery with zoom capability
- Product variations (size, color, headboard style)
- Price display with any offers
- Add to cart with quantity selector
- Delivery information
- Product specifications accordion
- Related products carousel

### 4. Shopping Cart
- Cart items list with images
- Quantity adjustment
- Remove items
- Order summary
- Promo code input
- Continue to checkout CTA

### 5. Checkout Flow
- Customer information form
- Delivery address
- Payment method selection (PayPal, Card, COD)
- Order review
- Confirmation page

### 6. About Us
- Brand story and values
- UK craftsmanship focus
- Quality materials section
- Team/workshop imagery

### 7. Contact Page
- Contact form
- Phone & email details
- Business address
- FAQ accordion
